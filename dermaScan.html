Process

How it works

Upload a photo. DermaScan runs three parallel analysis pipelines and returns a complete clinical explanation in under 10 seconds.

01 📸
Upload
Upload any photo of a skin lesion — phone photo or dermoscopy image. No account needed, no data stored.
02 🧠
Analyze
EfficientNet B0 runs inference. Simultaneously, a classical OpenCV pipeline computes ABCD clinical scores independently.
03 📊
Explain
Get a prediction, Grad-CAM heatmap, ABCD bar chart, and Monte Carlo uncertainty estimate — not just a number.
Explainability

Three-layer XAI pipeline

Most classifiers are black boxes. DermaScan explains every decision using three complementary methods aligned with clinical practice.

01 / VISUAL
Grad-CAM
Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping highlights which pixels drove the prediction. Red zones = high model attention. Shows the model is looking at the lesion, not the background.
hooks → model.features[-1]
backward pass → weighted activation map
02 / CLINICAL
ABCD Scoring
A parallel OpenCV pipeline scores each criterion independently using the same rule dermatologists use. Asymmetry, Border, Color variance, Diameter — all quantified.
Otsu segmentation → contour analysis
k-means color clustering → 4 scores
03 / UNCERTAINTY
MC Dropout
Dropout kept active during inference. 30 forward passes produce a prediction distribution. Low std = confident. High std = uncertain — refer to dermatologist regardless of prediction.
30 stochastic passes → μ ± σ
σ < 0.05 → High Confidence
